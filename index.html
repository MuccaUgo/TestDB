High-risk distinctions

IT strategy vs Digital business strategy

IT strategy is downstream and technical: how IT supports the chosen strategy. Digital business strategy is upstream and business-level: how the firm must evolve in a digital world.

Service models vs deployment models

Service models: IaaS, PaaS, SaaS. Deployment models: public, private, hybrid, community. Do not mix them: exam questions love this trap.

Supervised vs unsupervised vs reinforcement learning

Supervised uses labeled examples. Unsupervised discovers structure without labels. Reinforcement learns through actions and rewards.

Blockchain truth problem

Blockchain can make a record tamper-resistant, but it does not automatically make external input data true. This is one of the most important managerial limitations.

IoT data strategies

Process optimization improves internal efficiency/effectiveness. New generation improves future products. Customization adapts services to individuals. Data reselling monetizes collected data externally. Advertising & commerce supports sales, promotions or automatic reordering.

Upstream vs downstream Space Economy

Upstream builds and launches space infrastructure. Downstream uses satellite infrastructure/data to create Earth-based services and applications.
